Crystals Collector

You are given a random number at the start of the game.

There are five crystals at the bottom of the page. Each crystal has a hidden points value between 1 and 10.

By clicking on a crystal, you will add that crystal's number of points to your total score.

The goal is to match your total score to the random number.

If your total score matches the random number, you win!

If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

Each time you win or lose, the game automatically generates a new random number and a new points value for each crystal.
