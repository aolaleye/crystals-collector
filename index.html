Crystals Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

You will then see four crystals. The points value of each crystal is hidden until it is clicked on.

By clicking on a crystal, you will add that crystal's amount of points to your total score.

The goal is to match your total score to the random number provided at the beginning of the game.

If your total score matches the random number, you win!

If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

Each time a new game starts, the game will generate a new random number and the points value of each crystal will change.
